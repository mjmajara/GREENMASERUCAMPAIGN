Greener Maseru
Our Mission

Building a Cleaner City, Together

Environmental pollution has become a major challenge in Maseru. Plastic waste, illegal dumping, air pollution and poor waste management continue to damage our environment and affect public health. Streets filled with litter and blocked drainage systems create serious health risks for communities.

The Green Maseru Campaign responds to these challenges head-on — bringing together young people, students, businesses and local communities to create lasting change through collective action, education, and environmental responsibility.

Community Clean-Ups

Organized litter collection events across streets, parks, schools, and public spaces throughout Maseru.

Tree Planting

Restoring greenery and improving air quality by planting trees in schools, parks, and neighborhoods.

Recycling Education

Teaching communities how to separate and recycle plastics, paper, and glass to reduce pollution.

Social Media Awareness

Amplifying our message through #GreenMaseru, #KeepMaseruClean and digital engagement.
