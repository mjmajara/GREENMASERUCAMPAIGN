About the Campaign

Green Maseru Campaign 2026

A community-driven initiative transforming Maseru through clean-up activities, environmental education, recycling programs and the power of collective action.

Who We Are

A Movement Born from Community

The Green Maseru Campaign is a community-based initiative that focuses on promoting cleanliness, recycling, environmental education and sustainable living practices. The campaign encourages young people, students, businesses and local communities to work together to create a cleaner and healthier Maseru.

Environmental pollution has become a major challenge in Maseru. Plastic waste, illegal dumping, air pollution and poor waste management continue to damage the environment and affect public health. In response to these challenges, the Green Maseru Campaign was introduced as an environmental awareness initiative.

Our Approach

Hands-On Environmental Action

Through clean-up activities, tree planting, educational workshops and social media awareness, the campaign aims to inspire positive environmental change. Social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and WhatsApp play an important role in promoting the campaign.

Schools and youth groups are encouraged to participate in environmental competitions, debates, and awareness activities. Young people are the future leaders of society and involving them in environmental activities can create long-lasting change.

The campaign takes place across different communities around Maseru over a five-day period — in schools, public parks, streets, and community centers.

Campaign Goals

What We Stand For

🧹

Reduce Pollution

Reduce environmental pollution and encourage responsible waste management within communities across Maseru.

Environmental Education

Educate the public about the dangers of pollution and the importance of environmental protection and sustainable living.

Promote Recycling

Encourage recycling and reduce single-use plastics through educational programs and community recycling initiatives.

Youth Empowerment

Involve young people in environmental activities to build a generation that values sustainability and environmental care.

Community Unity

Strengthen community bonds through shared clean-up activities and tree-planting events that foster cooperation.

Economic Growth

Attract tourism and investment by making Maseru a cleaner, more attractive and sustainable city for all.
